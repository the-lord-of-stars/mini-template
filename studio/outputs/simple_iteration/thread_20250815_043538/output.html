Topic: evolution of research on sensemaking

Key Insights

Insight 1
The dataset shows a progression in the focus of research on sensemaking from 2010 to 2012. In 2010, the emphasis was on using social media for journalistic inquiry, highlighting the role of computational journalism and social media in sensemaking. By 2011, the focus shifted towards interaction with statistical models, emphasizing observation-level interaction and the integration of domain knowledge with data for insight generation. In 2012, the research further evolved to explore semantic interaction for sensemaking, focusing on user interaction, visualization, and analytic reasoning, indicating a trend towards more sophisticated user interactions and model steering in visual analytics.
